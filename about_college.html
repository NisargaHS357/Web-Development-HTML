S-VYASA DEEMED TO BE UNIVERSITY

SVYASA Yoga University is a renowned institution dedicated to the study and practice of yoga. It was established in 1987 by Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana (S-VYASA) in Bangalore, India. The university offers various courses and programs in yoga, including undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral degrees.SVYASA is known for its emphasis on research and holistic approach to yoga education, combining traditional wisdom with modern scientific understanding.
